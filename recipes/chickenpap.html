Chicken Paprikash

This chicken paprikash can be made either in an Instant PotÂ® or on the stovetop. No matter which method you use, it will be cooked in less than 30 minutes! Feel free to use a blend of paprika

  • 1(12 ounce) package egg noodles
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon minced parsley
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper, divided

  • 3 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 3 tablespoons paprika
